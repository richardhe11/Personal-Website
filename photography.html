PHOTOGRAPHY

Taking photos, for me at least, represents a level of aethestic awareness that I never knew I loved until I felt the weight of the shutter click for the first time. I take photos for a multitude of reasons: to capture the small beauties of the world around me, to remember my perceptions of the world from another time...

...but most importantly, to remind myself of the majesty that is our world.
