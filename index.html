A SUMMARY

I'm an artist because I find beauty in the everyday, and if I'm not going to share it with the world, who is?

Through different media, from prose to film, I strive to bring out the art in whatever I find. As a writer, I write with whimiscal detail and seek to present the art within the often-confused human mind. As a photographer, I collect rare, unique moments that are often overlooked. And as a web designer, I layer together all of these elements to create a multimedia collection of ideas.

As an artist, I seek to share the art I see with the world. This portfolio, if you'll call it that, is a collection of my finest dreams. Enjoy.
